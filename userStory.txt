User story for garage.com "announcements website"

Visitor: an individual who is searching the net,
             he is an unknown user, he is not yet a customer.
             his tasks: 
             - consult announcements
             - contact admin
             - create account

Customer: a visitor who has already created an account on the site.
              his tasks:
              - manage announcements
              - make an order
              - manage cart
              - user account management
              - contact admin

Admin: the webmaster, 
       ensures the dynamism of the site, watches over the updating of articles
       and manages the management of payments and deliveries.
              his tasks:
              - manage announcements
              - order management
              - user Management
              - admin account management
